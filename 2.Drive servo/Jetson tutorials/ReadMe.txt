The battery input interface of the drive board has an access voltage range of 5V-8.4V
The input voltage of the VCC pin of the drive board is 3.3V. It is forbidden to connect 5V, otherwise the drive board chip will be broken.
When connecting serial port or IIC wiring, only RX, TX, GND or SDA, SCL and GDN pins can be connected, and VCC (3.3V) can not be connected.
